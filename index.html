Baramulla: Sardi ka Qahar Jari, Agle 24 Ghanton mein Barfbaari ka Imkaan

Juma, 19 December 2025

Baramulla: Shumali Kashmir ke azla samet Baramulla mein shadeed sardi ki lehar jari hai. Mausamiyat vibhag (MeT) ke mutabiq, aane wale 24 se 48 ghanton mein pahadi ilaqon mein halki se darmiyani barfbaari ho sakti hai. Intezamia ne logon ko hoshiyar rehne aur sardi se bachne ke intezamat karne ki salaah di hai.

Bipin Rawat Stadium ko Milengi Day-Night Lights

Tareekh: 19 December 2025

Baramulla: DC Minga Sherpa ne elaan kiya hai ki Baramulla ke main stadium mein Rs 35 lakh ki laagat se char naye day-night lights lagayi jayengi. Isse March 2024 tak yahan raat mein bhi cricket aur football matches khele ja sakenge.

Baramulla mein 3 Naye 'Snow Control Rooms' Taiyaar

Tareekh: 19 December 2025

Baramulla: Sardiyon ki tayyari ke liye Sopore, Watergam aur Kreeri mein naye control rooms banaye gaye hain. Barf hatane ke liye 34 naye machines ko alag-alag jaghon par deploy kiya gaya hai taaki raaste band na hon.

Pattan: Rafiabad ke Rehne Wale Shakhs ki Mashkooq Laash Mili

Tareekh: 19 December 2025

Pattan: Rafiabad ke rehne wale 35 saala Javid Ahmad Mir ki laash Pattan ke Gousbug ilaqe mein mashkooq halat mein mili. Police ne mamla darj kar liya hai aur investigation shuru kar di hai. Ghar walon ne insaaf ki maang ki hai.
